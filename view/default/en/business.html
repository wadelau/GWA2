Business field

Self operation product

As a result of our customer feedback, to our feedback, BFD has researched many new products, and provides the service of chopping, baking and pasting of the imported materials such as American almonds, American pistachios, Turkish hazelnuts and Californian walnuts.

Packaging product

BFD owns the “Findik” brand packaging product, focusing on the R&D of leisure food. The sales channels are retail shops, supermarkets, department stores and wholesale markets. The main products are imported American almonds, Californian walnuts, Californian pistachios, Turkish hazelnuts, Turkish apricots, Vietnamese cashews and other domestic products. The nuts are usually salted and naturally roasted. We usually have 100-150g packed products and a separate gift box. At present, we have set a good sales channel in Beijing and other major cities. We expect to cooperate with old and new friends for a better future.

Agent Processing

According to individual requirements, we can provide the service of chopping, roasting, milling, pasting, classifying, vacuum packing, micro drying and sterilizing of hazelnuts, almonds, bitter almonds, pistachios, walnuts, macadamia peanuts and others. At the same time, we offer technical support and new product research business of hazelnuts, almonds, macadamias and other nuts.

Agent import and export

Beijing JP international owns the import and export license issued by China commercial ministry, Beijing Commercial Bureau and Chinese Customs Bureau. BFD has set a modern logistic platform for clearance, transportation, settlement of exchange etc. We provide the full service from customs clearance to inland transportation and door to door service. We will provide a safe, fast and standard import and export service.
