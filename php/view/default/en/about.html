About us

Beijing Findik Foods Co. Ltd. (hereafter referred to as BFD) is a subsidiary corporation of JPI Group Inc. Ltd. Adhering to its operation strategy that “Quality is First, Customer is Paramount” and having experienced years of practice, BFD has grown to be the largest hazelnut manufacturer, specializing in hazelnut R&D, production, sales and training.

BFD is located at the Huairou Yanxi Industrial Zone in Beijing. We have an excellent environment, advanced equipment, local processing, the best nut processing techniques, stable technical support and a good relationship with material suppliers. This guarantees the high quality of BFD’s hazelnut kernel, chopped hazelnut, hazelnut meal and hazelnut paste as well as ensuring the timeliness of supply. BFD has obtained the HACCP certification and ISO9001 system approvals.

Thanks to the group’s resources, stable customer relationship and rich experience in processing and sales, BFD is expanding to include other products, such as American almonds, pistachios, cranberry, blueberry, prune, Vietnamese cashews, Turkish apricot, figs, Australian macadamia and more. Sticking to the principle of advanced technology and independent development, BFD is increasing its investment in R&D to provide customers with high quality processed products and superior service.
